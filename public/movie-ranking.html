The Best Movies According to Erol

My top 3 movies of all time.

Interstellar

This is my all-time favorite sci-fi movie. The visuals and the concept of time and space really blow my mind.

The Matrix

A groundbreaking movie that challenges how we see reality. It's packed with action and deep philosophy.

The Dark Knight

One of the best superhero films ever made. The intense performances and dark themes make it unforgettable.
